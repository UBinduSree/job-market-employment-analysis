# Data dictionary

- job_title: Role title (e.g., Data Analyst). [web:43]
- company: Hiring company name. [web:43]
- location: City/Region (e.g., Bangalore, Remote). [web:43]
- industry: Industry string. [web:43]
- experience_level: Entry, Mid, Senior, etc. [web:43]
- employment_type: Full-time, Contract, etc. [web:43]
- salary_min: Numeric lower bound of salary. [web:43]
- salary_max: Numeric upper bound of salary. [web:43]
- posted_date: ISO date or parseable string. [web:43]
- skills: Comma-separated tags (e.g., Python, SQL, Excel). [web:43]
